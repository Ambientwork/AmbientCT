Features

Everything your practice needs

🏥

Full PACS Server

Orthanc with DICOMweb, C-STORE, and WADO. Receive images directly from any DICOM device on your network.

🧠

3D Volume Rendering

Axial, sagittal, and coronal MPR views powered by Cornerstone3D. GPU-accelerated WebGL rendering.

🦷

Dental Presets

Window/level presets optimized for bone, soft tissue, implants, mandibular canal, and airway analysis.

📦

One Command Deploy

Docker Compose on Mac, Linux, and Windows. From zero to running PACS in under 5 minutes.

🔒

Privacy First

Runs entirely on your LAN. No cloud. No tracking. No analytics. Your patient data stays yours.

📂

Any DICOM Source

Drag & drop files, use the CLI, or push from any CBCT, CT, or MRI scanner via DIMSE C-STORE.
